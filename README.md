## ðŸ’» The Software: Healthcare AI Studio

The "Healthcare AI Studio" is the core technical spine of the course. It is an open-access software platform developed at Harvard University that represents the cutting edge of agentic AI software development best practices.

**What It Is:**
*   A healthcare-specific adaptation of agentic AI standards.
*   An AI-native product development system.
*   Built around **multiple AI agents**, each representing expert roles on your team.

**Why It's Powerful:**
Participants do not just "prompt an LLM". They orchestrate a virtual expert council. The system enforces structured reasoning, consistent documentation, and repeatable decision-making.

## ðŸ“… Course Structure (Day-by-Day Arc)

This software supports the 5-day rapid build cycle of the course:

*   **Day 1: Seeing the System**
    *   Use AI agents to identify and vet high-leverage problems using health system diagnostic frameworks.
    *   *Output:* Concept Document.
*   **Day 2: Stress-Testing the Idea**
    *   AI-simulated expert panels (clinical, regulatory, operational).
    *   Multi-agent debate and critique on problem-solution fit.
*   **Day 3: From Idea to Product**
    *   Build a full Product Requirements Document (PRD).
    *   Validate assumptions using AI-simulated users.
*   **Day 4: Build, Test, Ship**
    *   AI-assisted software development and local testing.
    *   *Output:* Working AI-enabled prototype or MVP.
*   **Day 5: Growth & Demo**
    *   Go-to-market strategy, final demo, and pitch.
